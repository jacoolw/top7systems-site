Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

After downloading FrostDip, simply open the app and follow the on-screen instructions. The app will guide you through the initial setup.

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device and included in your iCloud device backups if enabled. We do not store your data on our servers.

The app is crashing or not working correctly

Please make sure you're running the latest version of the app and iOS. If the problem persists, try restarting the app or your device. If none of that helps, contact us below.

Contact Us
