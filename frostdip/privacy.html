FrostDip — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Introduction

Top 7 Systems ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FrostDip mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

FrostDip is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide the app's functionality:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to help us improve the app experience.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and OS version for compatibility purposes.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location unless you voluntarily provide it through our support channels.

Data Storage

All user-created content is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your personal data on external servers.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies. These may include Apple's App Store services and analytics frameworks.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@top7systems.net.
