Professional GPS Stamping

Every photo stamped with precise location data

Stampr burns GPS coordinates, street address, altitude, compass bearing, timestamp, and your project name directly onto each photo. GPS data is also embedded in the EXIF metadata, so your shots pin correctly in Photos, GIS software, and claims tools.

  • Decimal, DMS, UTM, and MGRS coordinate formats
  • Altitude in metres or feet, true or magnetic north
  • GPS accuracy colour-coding with optional fix lock
  • Capture time zone kept on the stamp and in EXIF
Voice Notes

Speak your observations. They're stamped on the photo.

Record a voice note after each shot. Stampr transcribes it on-device and stamps the text directly onto the image. On supported devices, on-device AI tidies the dictation into a clean caption with suggested tags. Cleanup only removes dictation artifacts, it can never invent content, and the verbatim transcript is always kept.

  • Hands-free mode for when you're wearing gloves
  • AI cleanup and auto-tags, fully on-device
  • Play back original audio anytime
Project Organisation

Every photo in its place. Every project at your fingertips.

Group photos into colour-coded projects. Set a default project so every new capture is filed automatically. Toggle between a photo grid and an interactive map to see exactly where each shot was taken. Sort newest or oldest first and filter by date, project, or tag.

  • Colour-coded projects with automatic filing
  • Interactive map view with location pins
  • Search across notes, addresses, and tags
  • Private iCloud sync on iPhone, so work carries to a new phone
Reports & Export

Professional PDF reports in one tap

Share stamped photos individually or in bulk. Stampr generates a professional PDF with embedded photos in chronological order, a map overview with numbered pins, accurate page numbering, and full metadata for every shot. Export project data as CSV, KML, or GeoJSON for spreadsheets, Google Earth, and GIS tools.

  • PDF reports with cover page, map, and metadata
  • CSV, KML, and GeoJSON export
  • Share project files (.stamprproj) with teammates

iPhone & Android

Two native apps. One workflow.

Stampr is built natively for each platform, with the same stamp format, projects, PDF reports, and exports on both. Here's what's specific to each.

Stampr for iPhone

Requires iOS 26.2 or later. Runs on iPhone and iPad.

  • Private iCloud sync: photos, projects, and voice notes carry to a new phone. Optional, switchable in Settings
  • On-device transcription with Apple Speech, AI cleanup with Apple Intelligence
  • Tap-to-focus with AE/AF lock, camera flip, off/auto/on flash, volume-button shutter
  • Action button, Siri, and Spotlight search of your notes
  • Stampr Pro: US $4.99 one-time

Stampr for Android

Requires Android 10 or later.

  • Local-only storage: nothing is uploaded anywhere, by design
  • On-device transcription with Whisper (one-time ~32 MB model download, works on every phone), AI cleanup on phones with on-device AI
  • Optional OpenStreetMap inset on the stamp, editable addresses
  • Quick Settings tile and home screen shortcuts
  • Stampr Pro: US $9.99 one-time

Project files travel with your team. Today project files move between iPhones and between Android phones; Stampr for Android already uses the cross-platform file format, and iPhone-to-Android exchange arrives with a coming iOS update.
