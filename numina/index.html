Questions parents ask

Is Numina really free?

Yes, completely free. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.

Does Numina work offline?

Yes. Everything works offline after you download it. Optional iCloud sync keeps a child's progress in step across their own iPhone and iPad.

What ages is Numina for?

Numina is designed for children roughly 8 to 11 years old, about grades 3 to 6, from first sums and number sense up to fractions, decimals and percentages.

Does Numina use AI to make the questions?

No. Every question, answer and hint is created by our own engine and checked for correctness, so the math is always right. There is no generative AI anywhere in the learning content.

Can more than one child use it?

Yes. Each child gets their own profile with separate progress, level, streak, medals and Sparks. Add or switch children in the grown-up area.

Is my child's data safe?

Yes. Numina collects no data and uses no third-party tracking or analytics. It is built to comply with children's-privacy laws like COPPA, and any sync uses your own private iCloud.
