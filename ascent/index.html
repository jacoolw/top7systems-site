The engine behind every problem

Ascent does not use generative AI and does not ship a fixed bank of worksheet questions. Every problem, hint, and worked solution comes from our own algebra engine, built for this app and checked to be exactly right.

🎲

Endless and fresh

Each of the 56 skills has its own generator that composes new problems on the spot. Practice a skill twice and you meet different numbers, while the difficulty stays true to where you are on the mountain.

⚖️

Exact to the last fraction

The engine computes in exact arithmetic: fractions stay fractions and radicals stay radicals, so 5/6 never becomes 0.83 and √8 is understood to equal 2√2. Answers are graded by mathematical equivalence, and any correctly simplified form counts.

🧭

One source of truth

The same engine that writes a problem also checks your answer, feeds the four radio hints, and walks the worked solution, with every step guaranteed to preserve the equation's solutions. Help can never contradict the math.

🩺

It knows the classic slips

Wrong answers are compared against the mistakes students actually make, like forgetting to flip an inequality sign. When yours matches one, Ascent names the slip and how to fix it instead of just saying "try again", and a matcher only fires when the mistake is unambiguous.

The whole course is machine-verified: in our test suite an automated learner climbs all 56 skills end to end, every route-test answer is re-derived independently, and the engine survives thousands of randomized stress tests before anything ships.

Room to work

Algebra is worked out, not guessed. Every problem in Ascent comes with a scratchpad, opened with a tap: open space to think, sketch, and show your steps before you commit to an answer.

✏️

Draw it or type it

Sketch your working with a finger or Apple Pencil, using a pen, highlighter, and eraser. Or switch to type and jot rough notes. Whatever helps you think.

🙈

Yours alone

The app never reads or grades what you write here. It is thinking space, not an answer box, so there is no wrong way to use it.

🧽

Fresh every problem

Like real scrap paper, it wipes clean when you move on. And it is there on every single problem, route tests included.

Questions climbers ask

When does Ascent launch?

Soon. The whole mountain is built and climbable end to end, from Lukla to the summit; we're in final testing and App Store preparation. Email us and we'll send you one note on launch day.

How much will it cost?

Free to start: the route through Camp One (foundations, the language of algebra, and all of linear equations) costs nothing. A single one-time purchase, the Full Expedition, unlocks the rest of the mountain. No subscription, and it's family shareable.

Who is Ascent for?

Anyone taking or retaking Algebra 1, roughly ages 13 and up. Already partway through algebra? An optional placement check at the start, the flight in, can drop you as high as Camp Two, so you never grind through material you already own. For ages 8 to 11, we make Numina, our arithmetic game.

Is it a complete Algebra 1 course?

Yes. Fifty-six skills across twelve legs cover a full US Algebra 1 course: equations, inequalities, functions, systems, exponents, polynomials, factoring, quadratics, and radicals, finishing with the three-part Summit Day mixed review. Reaching the summit means finishing the course.

Does Ascent use AI to make the questions?

No. Every problem, hint, and worked solution comes from our own exact algebra engine and is checked for correctness, so the math is always right. There is no generative AI anywhere in the learning content.

Is it private? Does it work offline?

Yes and yes. No ads, no tracking, no third-party code, no data collection. Everything works offline, and optional sync runs through your own private iCloud.
